How to Play

Objective

Move all the cards from the Nertz pile to the common play area (Lake).

Layout

  • Demon Pile: 13 cards, face-down.
  • Stock: Remaining deck after setting up Nertz and River, face-down.
  • River: Four piles beside the Nertz pile, each starting with one card face-up.
  • Lake (Common Area): Empty at the start; multiple shared foundations begin here where players play cards.

Allowed Moves

  • Players rapidly play cards from their River and Nertz pile into the Lake, building up from Ace to King in matching suits.
  • Cards from the hand can replenish the River as spaces appear.
  • Cards are moved between River piles in descending order and alternating colors.
  • Only Kings (or sequences starting with a King) can be moved to an empty River pile.
  • If the Lake has no suitable cards, players can draw from the Stock to the River.

Scoring

Players score one point for each card successfully moved to the Lake.
At the end of each round, players lose two points for every card left in their Nertz pile.
Rounds continue until one player reaches a predetermined score or the players agree to end the game.
