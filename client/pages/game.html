How to Play

Objective

Move all the cards from the Demon pile to the common play area (Lake).

Starting Layout

  • Demon Pile: 13 cards, face-down.
  • Stock: Remaining deck after setting up Demon pile and River piles.
  • River: Four piles beside the Demon pile, each starting with one card, face-up.
  • Lake (Common Area): Empty at the start; multiple shared foundations begin here where all players play cards.

Allowed Moves

  • Players play cards from their River and Demon pile into the Lake, building up from Ace to King in matching suits.
  • Cards from the Demon pile can fill the River as empty spaces appear.
  • Cards are only placed on River piles in descending order and alternating colors.
  • Only Kings (or sequences starting with a King) and cards from the demon pile can be moved to an empty River pile.
  • If the Lake has no suitable cards, players can draw from the Stock to the River.

Scoring

Players score one point for each card successfully moved to the Lake.
At the end of the game, players lose one point for each card in their Demon pile.
Rounds continue until one player reaches a predetermined score or the players agree to end the game.
